A collection of related records attached to a task. Sidecar collections include todos, links, requirements, plans, questions, runs, changes, artifacts, handoffs, and audit data. Each collection lives in a subdirectory of the task bundle directory. Defined in `taskledger/domain/sidecars.py`.
